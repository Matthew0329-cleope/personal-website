My Kind of Film

TV Shows

"Batang Quiapo" follows Tanggol, a young man surviving the streets of Quiapo, Manila, as he grapples with his identity, crime, and redemption.

Title: Batang Quiapo


"Pilipinas Got Talent" is a reality competition showcasing Filipinos' extraordinary talents, from singing to acrobatics, with the chance to win fame and fortune.

Title: Pilipinas Got Talent


"World of Dance Philippines" features dancers competing in different styles, judged by professionals, aiming for the title of the country's best dance act.

Title: World of Dance

Movies

"Real Steel" is a sci-fi sports drama where a former boxer and his son rebuild a discarded robot to compete in high-stakes robotic boxing matches.

Title: Real Steel


"The Karate Kid" tells the story of a young boy learning kung fu from a wise mentor, facing bullies, and proving himself in a martial arts tournament.

Title: The Karate Kid


"Avengers: Endgame" is the grand conclusion to the Infinity Saga, where the remaining heroes unite for a final battle to undo Thanos' catastrophic snap.

Title: Avengers: Endgame
